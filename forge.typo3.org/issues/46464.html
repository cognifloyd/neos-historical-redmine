Bug #46464

Remove Neos SiteRepository from NodeConverter

Added by Soren Malling over 2 years ago. Updated over 1 year ago.

Status:Resolved Start date:2013-03-20
Priority:Should have Due date:
Assigned To:- % Done:

100%
Category:-
Target version:-

Description

ATM TYPO3.TYPO3CR packages in only useable along with the TYPO3.Neos package, since the Neos SiteRepository it required in the typeconverter NodeConverter

/**
 * @Flow\Inject
 * @var \TYPO3\Neos\Domain\Repository\SiteRepository
         */
        protected $siteRepository;

Solution (I guess):
-------------------
Move the NodeConverter to TYPO3.Neos or remove dependency on the neos siterepository

  • or declare the dependency in composer.json

Description

Pages and Content Elements are used in Presentation tier of TYPO3. The palettes and data types for Content Element sadly are not state of the art and future proof at the moment. Several properties for Pages and Content Elements are not needed anymore these days - others are required.

Deliver strong defaults for the core Content Types.
Also TYPO3 will need to allow different rendering for Content Elements in Presentation.
